About

I work on multimodal and temporal deep learning spanning clinical time-series modeling (EHR), medical imaging and computational pathology, and video understanding. My recent work includes robust representation learning over time, generative modeling for computational staining/destaining, and video-based action recognition and captioning.

Last updated: 2026

Research

My research focuses on learning reliable, interpretable representations from multimodal data, with an emphasis on temporal modeling. I develop methods for clinical decision support using EHR trajectories, generative modeling for computational pathology pipelines, and video understanding for action recognition and captioning.
